# Peru 2017 Coastal Nino

**Event ID:** `MHW_PERU_2017`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2017-01-01 to 2017-05-31 (150 days)
**Region:** -20° to -5° latitude, -85° to -75° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.0°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
catastrophic flooding on land, anchoveta fishery disruption, mortality of juvenile fish

## Physical drivers
coastal El Niño pattern with strong Kelvin-wave forcing

## Mechanism
relaxation of upwelling winds and warm-water intrusion to coast

## Scientific citation
Garreaud 2018 (IJ Climatology)
