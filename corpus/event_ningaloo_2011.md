# Ningaloo Nino 2011

**Event ID:** `MHW_NINGALOO_2011`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2010-12-01 to 2011-05-31 (181 days)
**Region:** -30° to -15° latitude, 110° to 120° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.0°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
mass coral bleaching on Ningaloo Reef; whale shark aggregation disrupted; kelp regime shift

## Physical drivers
strong Leeuwin Current driven by La Niña; record rainfall

## Mechanism
enhanced southward advection of warm tropical water under La Niña

## Scientific citation
Feng et al. 2013 (Sci Rep)
