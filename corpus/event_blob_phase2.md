# NE Pacific Blob (phase 2)

**Event ID:** `MHW_BLOB_PHASE2`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2014-10-01 to 2016-04-30 (577 days)
**Region:** 35° to 55° latitude, -160° to -125° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.8°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
unprecedented harmful algal blooms, Dungeness crab fishery closures, forage fish collapse

## Physical drivers
continuation of the 2013-14 Blob, reinforced by the strong 2015-16 El Niño

## Mechanism
re-intensification through winter due to persistent positive PDO and El Niño teleconnections

## Scientific citation
Di Lorenzo & Mantua 2016 (Nat CC)
