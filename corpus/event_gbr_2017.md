# Great Barrier Reef bleaching 2017

**Event ID:** `MHW_GBR_2017`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2017-02-01 to 2017-05-31 (119 days)
**Region:** -24° to -10° latitude, 142° to 155° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.5°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
additional 20% coral mortality on top of 2016 losses

## Physical drivers
back-to-back event with 2016; no recovery window

## Mechanism
thermal stress on already-weakened reef; some recovery-phase corals killed

## Scientific citation
Hughes et al. 2018 (Science)
