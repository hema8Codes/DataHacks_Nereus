# Tasman Sea 2015-16

**Event ID:** `MHW_TASMAN_2015`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2015-09-01 to 2016-03-31 (212 days)
**Region:** -50° to -30° latitude, 150° to 175° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.9°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
mass mortality in abalone, sea urchin, and reef fish populations; documented poleward range shifts

## Physical drivers
East Australian Current extension strengthened, transporting subtropical water south

## Mechanism
subtropical water advection under a blocking high-pressure pattern

## Scientific citation
Oliver et al. 2017 (Nat Comms)
