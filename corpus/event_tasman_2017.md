# Tasman Sea 2017-18

**Event ID:** `MHW_TASMAN_2017`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2017-11-01 to 2018-02-28 (119 days)
**Region:** -50° to -30° latitude, 150° to 175° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.7°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
second major bleaching event for Tasman kelp forests; documented shifts in commercial lobster fishery

## Physical drivers
similar East Australian Current anomaly as 2015-16; atmospheric blocking persisted

## Mechanism
re-emergence of warm subtropical waters with atmospheric reinforcement

## Scientific citation
Perkins-Kirkpatrick et al. 2019
