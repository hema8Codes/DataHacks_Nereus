# North Atlantic 2023

**Event ID:** `MHW_NATL_2023`
**Category:** Extreme (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2023-05-01 to 2023-12-31 (244 days)
**Region:** 40° to 65° latitude, -60° to -10° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +4.5°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
bleaching in Florida Keys, Atlantic hurricane-season implications, disruption of Gulf of Maine cod fishery

## Physical drivers
reduced Saharan dust transport (less aerosol cooling), weakened Atlantic meridional overturning

## Mechanism
combination of aerosol forcing reduction and ocean-circulation slowdown

## Scientific citation
Kuhn et al. 2024 (Comms Earth Env)
