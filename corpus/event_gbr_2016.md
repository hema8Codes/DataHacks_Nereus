# Great Barrier Reef bleaching 2016

**Event ID:** `MHW_GBR_2016`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2016-02-01 to 2016-05-31 (120 days)
**Region:** -24° to -10° latitude, 142° to 155° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.8°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
29% of GBR shallow-water corals killed in 2016 alone (Hughes et al. 2017)

## Physical drivers
strong El Niño, global ocean heat content peak

## Mechanism
sustained heat stress exceeded 8-week bleaching thermal threshold (DHW > 8)

## Scientific citation
Hughes et al. 2017 (Nature)
