# Gulf of Maine 2016 (ongoing series)

**Event ID:** `MHW_GULFMAINE_2016`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2016-01-01 to 2016-12-31 (365 days)
**Region:** 42° to 45° latitude, -70° to -65° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.2°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
cod fishery collapse widely attributed to this warming regime; lobster shifting north into Canadian waters

## Physical drivers
ongoing warming trend punctuated by multi-month anomalies; one of the fastest-warming large marine ecosystems globally

## Mechanism
combination of long-term warming and intermittent heatwave intensification

## Scientific citation
Pershing et al. 2015 (Science)
