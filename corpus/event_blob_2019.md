# NE Pacific MHW 2019 (Blob 2.0)

**Event ID:** `MHW_BLOB_2019`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2019-06-01 to 2020-02-29 (273 days)
**Region:** 35° to 55° latitude, -160° to -125° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.0°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
less media attention than the 2013-15 Blob, but peak intensity was actually higher (+3.0°C); fisheries managers described it as 'Blob 2.0'

## Physical drivers
atmospheric high-pressure ridge over the Gulf of Alaska reduced mixing for 5+ months

## Mechanism
same shallow-MLD trapping pattern as the original, in a warming baseline

## Scientific citation
Amaya et al. 2020 (Nat Comms)
