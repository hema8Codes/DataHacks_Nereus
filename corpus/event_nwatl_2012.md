# North West Atlantic 2012

**Event ID:** `MHW_NWATL_2012`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2012-02-01 to 2012-09-30 (242 days)
**Region:** 35° to 50° latitude, -75° to -50° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.0°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
American lobster northward range shift, Gulf of Maine cod recruitment collapse (Mills et al. 2013)

## Physical drivers
persistent atmospheric ridge kept cold-air outbreaks away from the shelf

## Mechanism
reduced wintertime air-sea heat loss; stratification persisted into spring

## Scientific citation
Mills et al. 2013 (Oceanography)
