# Region: Tasman Sea

## Coverage
50-30°S latitude, 150-175°E longitude. Includes the Tasmanian coast and the southern portion of the East Australian Current.

## Why it matters for Nereus
The Tasman Sea has experienced back-to-back Severe MHWs in 2015-16 and 2017-18. It is one of the top-ten fastest-warming regions globally and is a living laboratory for poleward species redistribution.

## Ecological stakes
Abalone, rock lobster, kelp forest-dependent species. Tasmanian abalone fishery is a signature commercial operation with annual value approximately AUD $100M.

## Driver pattern
East Australian Current extension intensification, frequently linked to atmospheric blocking. The ENSO-Tasman connection is weaker than in the tropics.
