# Mediterranean 2022

**Event ID:** `MHW_MED_2022`
**Category:** Extreme (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2022-05-15 to 2022-09-15 (123 days)
**Region:** 30° to 45° latitude, -5° to 40° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +4.3°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
mass mortality in gorgonian corals and Posidonia seagrass meadows; peak +4.3°C is among the highest marine anomalies ever recorded

## Physical drivers
atmospheric heat dome over southern Europe coupled with reduced Atlantic inflow

## Mechanism
semi-enclosed basin with limited heat export, under extended heatwave-over-land conditions

## Scientific citation
Marullo et al. 2023 (Sci Tot Env)
