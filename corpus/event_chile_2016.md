# Chile / SE Pacific 2016 MHW-HAB

**Event ID:** `MHW_CHILE_2016`
**Category:** Extreme (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2016-01-01 to 2016-04-30 (120 days)
**Region:** -45° to -20° latitude, -85° to -70° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.3°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
harmful algal bloom killed approximately 27 million farmed salmon, roughly $800 million in losses, per León-Muñoz et al. 2018

## Physical drivers
very strong El Niño (ONI +2.21) combined with positive PDO (+1.89) — extreme climate-mode configuration

## Mechanism
prolonged warm-water intrusion triggered Pseudochattonella algal bloom; anoxic conditions in aquaculture net pens

## Scientific citation
Leon-Munoz et al. 2018 (Sci Rep)
