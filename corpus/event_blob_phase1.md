# NE Pacific Blob (phase 1)

**Event ID:** `MHW_BLOB_PHASE1`
**Category:** Severe (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2013-11-01 to 2014-09-30 (333 days)
**Region:** 35° to 55° latitude, -160° to -125° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +2.5°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
salmon collapse on the west coast, sea lion starvation events in California, shift in tuna and sardine distributions northward

## Physical drivers
unusually weak winter storm activity led to reduced surface cooling; positive PDO amplified the anomaly

## Mechanism
a shallow mixed layer (~8.9 m shallower than baseline) trapped surface heat, preventing normal wintertime cooling

## Scientific citation
Bond et al. 2015 (GRL)
