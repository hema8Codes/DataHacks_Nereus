# Nereus user: marine scientist / Scripps researcher

## Profile
Academic or government researcher studying marine heatwaves, fisheries oceanography, or climate impacts. Publishes in GRL, Nature Communications, Journal of Marine Systems.

## Pain
Spends significant time on data plumbing — pulling OISST, subsetting Argo, querying iNaturalist, cross-referencing climate indices. Loses analytical productivity to file management.

## What Nereus gives them
A unified, queryable view across multiple oceanographic and biological datasets. Natural-language retrieval replaces multi-hop data engineering for common analytical questions.

## Why it's valuable
Research productivity. Not a direct revenue line but a credibility builder — if scientists use Nereus, the product has technical legitimacy.

## Secondary value
Nereus's retrospective analog search could seed new publications on MHW predictability, ecosystem response, and climate-mode teleconnections.
