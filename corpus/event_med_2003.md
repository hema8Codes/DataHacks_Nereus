# Mediterranean 2003 (historic)

**Event ID:** `MHW_MED_2003`
**Category:** Extreme (Hobday et al. 2016 classification)
**Period:** 2003-06-01 to 2003-09-30 (121 days)
**Region:** 30° to 45° latitude, -5° to 40° longitude
**Peak SST anomaly:** +3.5°C above the 1982-2011 climatology

## What happened
50+ species suffered mass mortalities down to 40 m depth (Garrabou et al. 2009); reference event for Mediterranean MHW science

## Physical drivers
European heatwave summer; atmospheric heat transfer to shallow coastal waters

## Mechanism
shallow coastal water rapidly equilibrates with extreme atmospheric temperatures

## Scientific citation
Garrabou et al. 2009 (Global CB)

## Context
This event is one of 15 canonical marine heatwaves in the Nereus reference catalog. Each event in the catalog is sourced from peer-reviewed literature and carries a bounding box, Hobday category, and peak SST anomaly for automated analog retrieval.
