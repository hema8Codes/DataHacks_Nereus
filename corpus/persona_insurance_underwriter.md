# Nereus user: parametric climate insurance underwriter

## Profile
Works at a specialty insurer (e.g., Swiss Re, Munich Re, or a climate-focused MGA). Writes parametric policies that pay out when SST exceeds a defined trigger for a defined duration.

## Pain
Prices parametric marine policies based on coarse historical frequency estimates. Lacks a defensible model for regional MHW probability conditional on climate-mode state. Cannot price fine-grained geographic differentiation.

## What Nereus gives them
A historical catalog of MHW events with Hobday-standard metadata (duration, peak anomaly, category), searchable by region, combined with the climate-mode state at event onset. Enables historical-analog-based pricing and underwriting.

## Why it's valuable
Parametric climate insurance is a fast-growing product line (>25% CAGR). A better underwriting model is worth tens of basis points on loss ratio, which at scale translates to millions in profit.

## Target accounts
Swiss Re, Munich Re, Hiscox climate desks, Jupiter Intelligence, and MGAs focused on agriculture and aquaculture risk.
